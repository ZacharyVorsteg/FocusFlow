Features

  • Simple, distraction-free timer interface
  • Customizable session durations (15, 25, 45, 60, 90 minutes)
  • Session labels for different work types (Focus, Deep Work, Study, etc.)
  • Daily and weekly statistics with visual charts
  • Session history with detailed logs
  • Works completely offline - no account required
  • All data stored locally on your device

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a focus session?

Open the app and tap the play button to start your timer. You can customize the duration and label before starting.

How do I change the session duration?

Tap the clock icon next to the play button to select from preset durations (15, 25, 45, 60, or 90 minutes).

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your app data will be included in your device backups.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Clear All Sessions" to delete your session history, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Can I track different types of work?

Yes! You can choose from preset labels like Focus, Deep Work, Study, Reading, Writing, and Coding in the Settings.
